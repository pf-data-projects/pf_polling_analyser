Instructions

If you are not yet logged in, please log in here.

Once you are logged in, follow the following steps:

  1. Click on the link to weight the survey results.

  2. A loading icon will appear on this page's tab while the data is weighted.

  3. Once the data is weighted you will be redirected here. You can then click on the link to download the data.

  4. Click on the link to calculate response data.

  5. A loading icon will appear on this page's tab while the responses for each question is calculated.

  6. Once that is completed you will be redirected here. You can then click on the link to download the data.

  7. Click on the link to run the table-maker.

  8. A loading icon will appear on this page's tab while the tables are generated.

  9. Once that is completed you will be redirected here. You can then click on the link to download the data.
