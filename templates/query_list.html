Instructions

If you are not yet logged in, please log in here.

Once you are logged in, follow the following steps:

  1. Click on the link to upload and weight the survey results.

  2. A loading icon will appear on this page's browser tab while the data is weighted.

  3. Once the data is weighted you will be redirected here. You can then click on the link to download the weighted data.

  4. Click on the link to upload data, specify crossbreaks, and calculate response percentages.

  5. A loading icon will appear on this page's browser tab while the responses for each question are calculated.

  6. Once that is completed you will be redirected here. You can then click on the link to download the data.

  7. Click on the link to upload calculated data and run the table-maker.

  8. A loading icon will appear on this page's browser tab while the tables are generated.

  9. Once that is completed you will be redirected here. You can then click on the link to download the data.
